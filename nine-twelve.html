Ninth to Twelfth Grades
14 to 18 year olds (High School)

Key advice: Students and their families should schedule a ‘team’ meeting with a guidance counselor in 9th grade to find out about college and other post-high school options, about high school graduation requirements and timeframes, and to develop a ‘game plan’ to achieve their post-high school goals. Remember that a college education is possible for all students regardless of their immigration status or income.

Important Dates
2015 Late Spring: 9th graders who want to change schools/reapply for high school admissions: High School Directory is released listing schools that accept new applicants for 10th grade
Summer: 10 and 11th graders: Attend summer college information sessions, open houses, tours, and prepare for standardized college admissions tests (PSAT, SAT, ACT)
Fall: First Day of School: September 9, 2015 Pre-K general enrollment period ends Friday, April 24, 2015 (Round One)
12th graders: Application deadline for CUNY spring enrollment (usually early September: you should have applied for financial aid before this date); start preparing applications for the many colleges that have a December 1st application deadline. Charter school enrollment period are Various. Please contact these schools directly
9th graders who want to change schools/reapply for high schools admissions: high school admissions process starts
10th and 11th graders: take SATs, PSATs (speak to a guidance counselor about the testing schedule that best suits your college or post-graduation plans)
12th graders: application deadlines for fall admissions at many colleges; application deadline for CUNY Macaulay Honors Program (usually in December); research and apply for scholarships. Many scholarships offer workshops and other assistance to applicants to prepare a strong application. A good resource for scholarship opportunities is the Jaime Lucero Mexican Studies Institute at Lehman College and CUNY’s new Dreamers’ Hub.
12th graders who want to apply for spring 2016 admissions to CUNY: fill out financial aid applications

2016 January: 12th graders who want to apply for fall 2016 admissions to CUNY: fill out financial aid applications (after January 1)
February: 12th graders Application deadline for CUNY fall enrollment
March: 9th graders who want to change schools/reapply for high school admissions: high school admissions general process Round 1 notification letters sent out; participate in general process Round 2 fairs and admissions
May: 9th graders who want to change schools/reapply to high school admissions: high school admissions general process Round 2 results and Appeals Round starts
School starts: September 2016 (exact date still undecided)
