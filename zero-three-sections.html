Babies and Toddlers, 0 to 3 years old

    The purpose of this section is:

    • To provide basic information about the development of babies and toddlers
    • To suggest specific steps you can take to help children of this age to fully develop, while building a solid base for their formal education in the following years
    • To supply information about available public programs in New York City that are useful for babies and toddlers of up to 3 years old
    • To provide online links to additional resources that you can consult in order to clarify doubts and get more detailed information
